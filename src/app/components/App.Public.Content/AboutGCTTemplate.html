Global Capital Trends® is Real Capital Analytics’ main report on the worldwide commercial real estate industry. Global Capital Trends® summarizes recent investment activity and trends for all of the major property types. The report also regularly includes rankings of leading deals, buyers, sellers and brokers.

Each issue of Global Capital Trends® includes a wealth of data delivered to your inbox, including:

  • Volume and pricing
  • Investor composition and leading buyers
  • Transaction structures
  • Niche property types

Global Capital Trends® subscribers receive the latest information about the global commercial real estate markets, reported on by industry experts. No longer do you need to go searching for this important information—now it is delivered directly to you, from a name you trust.
