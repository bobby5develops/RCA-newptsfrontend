Property Trade Search™
Our Premier, Global Transactional Commercial Real Estate Database

Online access to our proprietary database complete with buyers, sellers, brokers, lenders, terms and key property attributes…all with RCA’s proven commitment to data integrity.

Given any market globally, users can instantly survey property investment activity. This industry-leading data can help compare pricing and cap rates, or see the most active buyers, sellers and brokers in that market. Graphs and statistics are also readily available.

Subscribers can easily produce maps with a Comparable Set of recent transactions, or comprehensive Property Detail reports.

Further information is available on over 150,000+ Investor Profiles, that include key contact information, complete transaction history and known holdings.

Property Trade Search features:
  • Comprehensive data for over $10 trillion of CRE property transactions
  • QuickSearch from the homepage featuring Market/Property Type, Company, Address and CMBS QuickSearch
  • Access to Advanced Property Trade Search with additional comprehensive filters and search criteria
  • Instant property lists with additional filters and sorting criteria
  • Links to Property Detail pages as well as Investor Profiles
  • Property Details include market, building/land characteristics, transaction and player information, financing and history sections as well as geocoded photos
  • Exportability to PDF or Excel
