US Capital Trends®

Real Capital Analytics issues comprehensive reports summarizing recent investment activity and trends for each of the four core property types. Investment activity is analyzed collectively and arranged by property attributes, geographic markets and investment by capital sector.

New content is published regularly as the focus of the reports rotates through in-depth discussion of volume and pricing, the composition and depth of the investor base, transaction structures and sellers. Reports also include quarterly rankings of leading deals, buyers, sellers and brokers.


Regular topics include:

Investor Composition
Sellers & Net Investment
Volume & Pricing
Distressed Property Data
