Coverage

In 2000, Real Capital Analytics started tracking commercial property transactions in the United States. Beginning in 2007, coverage was expanded to include all markets globally. RCA has since then recorded a total of over $10 trillion in commercial real estate deals throughout 146 countries.

RCA focuses on the main property types: office, industrial, retail, apartment, hotel, development sites, and senior housing and care. RCA research is concentrated on commercial property and portfolio sales of $2.5 million or greater in the US, and $10 million or greater outside of the US.
