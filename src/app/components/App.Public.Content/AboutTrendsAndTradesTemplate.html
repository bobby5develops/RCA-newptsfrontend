Market Trends & Trades®

These comprehensive US market reports summarize recent investment activity and chart property sales trends for each of the four core property types (apartment, industrial, office and retail).

Note: upon the release of our Q4’10 data in 2011, these quarterly reports will be discontinued.

Each report features:

  • Quarterly statistics on investment sales and offerings
  • Cap rates and prices by property type, year built and closed price
  • Local and national comparisons
  • Average percent of ask price achieved
  • Average mortgage interest rates
  • Buyer and seller composition
  • Submarket volume statistics
  • Leading buyers, sellers, brokers and lenders
  • Largest recent deals

There are currently over 60 different Market Trends & Trades® reports available, all based on US data current through the most recently completed quarter.
